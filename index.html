Q1 · CTE Talent Pipeline

How can state agencies tap the CTE pipeline to fill IT roles and offer apprenticeships?
  • Define entry-level IT roles, e.g. Cyber Tech I or Help‑Desk Trainee, aligned with CTE curriculum.
  • Fast-track Youth Registered Apprenticeship approval: agreements are submitted within 10 calendar days of hire.
    MD Policy Issuance 2022-12
  • Leverage Apprenticeship Maryland: school coordinators match pre-screened juniors/seniors with state IT roles.
    HCPSS Apprenticeship Maryland
  • Blend work & study: 20 hrs/week (min 450 paid hours) plus a year of related classroom instruction.
    Policy Issuance 2023-10
  • Utilize Blueprint grants and state/federal tax credits to offset costs.
    MD Youth Apprenticeship FAQs
  • Proven: 2016 pilot (11 students) has grown to hundreds statewide, with local tracks in Frederick, Talbot, and Washington counties.
    Frederick Co. Apprenticeship  |  Talbot Co. CTE

Q2 · Government Career On‑Ramps (State Comparisons)

What are other states doing to connect students directly to government careers?
Colorado
  • Governor’s EO D 2022‑027: 50% apprenticeship increase, two new WBL programs per agency by 2025.
    Executive Order (PDF)
  • 300+ apprenticeship programs, 6,000+ participants, 7+ state agencies participating.
    Toolkit
North Carolina
  • ApprenticeshipNC placed 1,385 youth apprentices (16–25) with 155 employers in FY23–24.
    ApprenticeshipNC News
  • $12M state funding covers wage & tuition support, ~90% youth retention.
    Funding Report
Utah
  • Utah has 293 registered apprenticeship programs, 4,800+ active apprentices, ~90% retention.
    Apprenticeship Finder (Utah)

Q3 · Local‑First AI Strategy (Maryland Example)

What ideas can Maryland agencies use to build local‑first AI platforms and related training?
  • Governor Moore’s 2024 Executive Order created an AI Subcabinet and AI Enablement Roadmap for state agencies.
    AI Enablement Roadmap
  • Launched Office of AI Enablement at DoIT to govern, pilot, and operationalize secure AI.
    GovTech Coverage
  • Statewide AI training (InnovateUS) offered to every public employee.
    InnovateUS Training
  • Emphasis on in-state infrastructure and pilot-first scaling: data stays in Maryland, local talent leads.
    AI Roadmap
  • Credentialing pathways being built so agency staff and CTE students can earn "AI Operator" status.
    GovTech
  • All pilots are tested for privacy and bias—scale what works, share results statewide.
    AI Enablement Roadmap
