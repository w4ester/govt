MD Digital Government Summit
Will Forrester – Panel Questions, Answers & Resources

Panel Questions

  1. CTE Talent Pipeline: How can state agencies tap the CTE pipeline to fill IT roles and offer apprenticeships?
  2. Government Career On‑Ramps: What are other states doing to connect students directly to government careers?
  3. Local‑First AI Strategy: What ideas can Maryland agencies use to build local‑first AI platforms and related training?

Answers & Proof

Q1. CTE Talent Pipeline

  • Define entry-level roles that align with CTE skills, such as Cyber Tech I or Help‑Desk Trainee.
  • Fast-track Youth Registered Apprenticeship (Youth-RA) approval: agreements must be filed with DLLR within 10 calendar days of hire.
    MD Policy Issuance 2022-12 (PDF)
  • Pair via Apprenticeship Maryland: Juniors/seniors apply and are matched by school coordinators to public IT roles.
    HCPSS Apprenticeship Maryland
  • Blend work & study: Students work ~20 hours/week (total 450 paid hours) plus one year of related classroom instruction.
    MD Policy Issuance 2023-10 (PDF)
  • Leverage Blueprint grants & state/federal apprenticeship tax credits to offset agency wage costs.
    MD Youth Apprenticeship FAQs
  • Proven model: Since 2016, Maryland has expanded from 11 pilot students to hundreds statewide, with local CTE-aligned tracks in Frederick, Talbot, and Washington counties.
    Frederick County ApprenticeshipMD | Talbot County CTE Apprenticeship

Q2. Government Career On‑Ramps (State Comparisons)

Colorado
  • Governor’s Executive Order D 2022‑027 directs all agencies to increase registered apprenticeships by 50% and launch two new WBL programs per dept by 2025.
    Executive Order PDF
  • Over 300 apprenticeship programs with 6,000+ participants, including seven state agencies.
    Public Sector Apprenticeship Toolkit
North Carolina
  • ApprenticeshipNC placed 1,385 youth apprentices (ages 16–25) with 155 employers in FY 2023–24.
    ApprenticeshipNC News
  • State provided $12M in funding for wage and tuition support; retention rate for youth apprenticeships approaches 90%.
    ApprenticeshipNC Funding Report
Utah
  • Utah maintains 293 registered apprenticeship programs, 4,800+ apprentices, and a ~90% retention rate for completers.
    National Apprenticeship Finder (Utah)

Q3. Local‑First AI Strategy (Maryland Example)

  • Governor Moore’s 2024 Executive Order created an AI Subcabinet and mandated a 2025 “AI Enablement Strategy & Roadmap” for state agencies.
    Maryland AI Enablement Roadmap (PDF)
  • Office of AI Enablement (DoIT) launched to oversee secure, responsible deployment of state AI.
    GovTech AI Subcabinet Story
  • Maryland offers InnovateUS AI training to every state employee to grow AI literacy for pilots and full-scale projects.
    InnovateUS Training Coverage
  • Emphasis on in-state data control and pilot-first deployments, avoiding vendor lock-in and building in-house AI expertise.
    AI Enablement Roadmap
  • Developing credentialing pathways so public sector, CTE students, and agency staff can build and operate local AI.
    GovTech Story
  • All pilots are privacy/bias tested and only scaled if successful—“build local, scale what works.”
    AI Enablement Roadmap
