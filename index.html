Q1 · CTE Talent Pipeline

How can state agencies tap into the CTE talent pipeline to fill IT positions and offer apprenticeships?
  • Define entry-level IT roles, e.g. Cyber Tech I or Help‑Desk Trainee, aligned with CTE curriculum.
  • Fast-track Youth Registered Apprenticeship approval: agreements are submitted within 10 calendar days of hire.
    MD Policy Issuance 2022-12
  • Leverage Apprenticeship Maryland: school coordinators match pre-screened juniors/seniors with state IT roles.
    HCPSS Apprenticeship Maryland
  • Maryland's Division of College and Career Readiness provides statewide coordination for CTE programs and pathways.
    MD Division of College & Career Readiness
  • Blend work & study: 20 hrs/week (min 450 paid hours) plus a year of related classroom instruction.
    Policy Issuance 2023-10
  • Utilize Blueprint grants and state/federal tax credits to offset costs.
    MD Youth Apprenticeship FAQs
  • Proven: 2016 pilot (11 students) has grown to hundreds statewide, with local tracks in Frederick, Talbot, and Washington counties.
    Frederick Co. Apprenticeship  |  Talbot Co. CTE

Q5 · State IT Talent Gap Solutions

If we look beyond AI and the workforce talent and skills needed there, what have you seen other states do to close the gap in their overall IT talent gap?
Colorado
  • Governor's EO D 2022‑027: 50% apprenticeship increase, two new WBL programs per agency by 2025.
    Executive Order (PDF)
  • 300+ apprenticeship programs, 6,000+ participants, 7+ state agencies participating.
  • Colorado Apprenticeships Hub provides centralized resources and employer tools.
    Colorado Apprenticeships Hub
North Carolina
  • ApprenticeshipNC placed 1,385 youth apprentices (16–25) with 155 employers in FY23–24.
    ApprenticeshipNC News
  • $12M state funding covers wage & tuition support, ~90% youth retention.
    Funding Report
  • Main apprenticeship portal with data and employer resources.
    ApprenticeshipNC Main Site

Q3 · AI Local-First Strategy

🎯 30-Second Sound Bite:
"Maryland's AI local-first strategy means three things: data stays here, talent grows here, and innovation happens here. We've launched an AI Subcabinet and Office of AI Enablement at DoIT. Every state employee can access AI training through InnovateUS. We're building credentialing pathways so CTE students and staff become certified 'AI Operators.' The approach is pilot-first—test for privacy and bias, scale what works, share statewide. By training AI on our workflows and certifying our people, Maryland becomes the model—not the follower."
How could Maryland agencies utilize an AI local-first strategy to develop both talent and engineering and model for other states and institutions?
  • Governor Moore's 2024 Executive Order created an AI Subcabinet and AI Enablement Roadmap for state agencies.
    AI Enablement Roadmap
  • Launched Office of AI Enablement at DoIT to govern, pilot, and operationalize secure AI.
    GovTech Coverage
  • Statewide AI training (InnovateUS) offered to every public employee.
    InnovateUS Training
  • Emphasis on in-state infrastructure and pilot-first scaling: data stays in Maryland, local talent leads.
    AI Roadmap
  • Credentialing pathways being built so agency staff and CTE students can earn "AI Operator" status.
    GovTech
  • All pilots are tested for privacy and bias—scale what works, share results statewide.
    AI Enablement Roadmap

Mentorship, Training & Upskilling

In what ways can mentorship, training programs, and apprenticeships support continuous upskilling?

  • Mentorship pairs early-career staff or students with experienced professionals—building skills, confidence, and networks on the job.
  • Training programs (like NC ApprenticeshipNC or MD InnovateUS) provide formal, stackable learning for both technical and soft skills.
  • Apprenticeships blend paid real-world work and targeted instruction, letting learners develop, practice, and continually upgrade in-demand skills.
  • Colorado's model integrates career coaches and ongoing mentorship throughout the apprenticeship placement process.
    Colorado Apprenticeships Hub
  • Maryland's CTE youth pathways assign workplace mentors to every apprentice for targeted support.
    MD CTE Youth Apprenticeship
  • These models make upskilling part of daily work—helping staff and students build credentials, earn while learning, and adapt to evolving public-sector needs.
